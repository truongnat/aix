# Engineering Management Pro

Expert-level orchestration of engineering teams and technical delivery. Focuses on balancing people development with technical excellence and business impact.

## Boundary

**`engineering-management-pro`** covers Team Building (Hiring, Onboarding), People Development (1:1s, Mentorship), Technical Strategy (Architecture review, Tech debt), and SDLC Operations (Agile, Metrics). It does NOT cover individual coding tasks (use specific framework skills for that).

## When to use

- Designing a high-performing engineering team structure.
- Leading a technical strategy or refactoring roadmap.
- Mentoring engineers and designing career progression frameworks.
- Optimizing SDLC processes (Agile, DORA metrics).

## Workflow

1. **Alignment**: Align technical goals with business objectives.
2. **Strategy**: Define a technical roadmap and architecture direction.
3. **Execution**: Facilitate Agile rituals and manage delivery velocity.
4. **Mentorship**: Conduct 1:1s and support professional growth.
5. **Optimization**: Analyze DORA metrics and identify process bottlenecks.
6. **Communication**: Manage stakeholder expectations and technical debt reporting.

### Operating principles

- **People First**: Focus on the team's growth and health as the foundation for success.
- **Output over Activity**: Focus on business impact and delivery rather than just task completion.
- **Radical Candor**: Provide direct, empathetic feedback to drive excellence.
- **Karpathy Principles**: Think before coding (Planning), Simplicity first, Surgical changes, Goal-driven execution.

## Quick example

**Success Metrics:**
- Reduction in production bugs.
- Increase in Developer Experience (DX) survey scores.
- Improved CI/CD pipeline reliability.

## Checklist before calling the skill done

- [ ] **Think Before Coding**: Technical strategy and people impact analyzed before making changes.
- [ ] **Simplicity First**: Simple, lightweight processes used over complex bureaucracy.
- [ ] **Surgical Changes**: Process changes are introduced incrementally to minimize disruption.
- [ ] **Goal-Driven Execution**: Delivery and health metrics (DORA) defined and tracked.
- [ ] Career progression and mentorship frameworks established.
- [ ] Agile operations (Scrum/Kanban) optimized for team flow.
- [ ] Technical debt identified and prioritized in the roadmap.
